    Definition of ESI descriptor

    Symbols Descriptor
    S(ρUV) Similarity in UV/vis spectra
    S(ρex) Similarity in density of electronically excited states (ρex)
    S(ρorb) Similarity in density of molecular orbitals (ρorb)
    S(ρIR) Similarity in IR spectra (ρIR)
    S(ρfreq) Similarity in density of vibrational states (ρfreq)
    IPv Vertical ionization potential from S0
    λS0→C Structural relaxation energy in C after ionization
    λC→S0 Structural relaxation energy in S0 after deionization at C
    EAv Vertical electron affinity from S0
    λS0→A Structural relaxation energy in A after anionization (electron-attachment)
    λA→S0 Structural relaxation energy in S0 after deionization at A
    ∆EST,v Energy of vertical transition from S0 to T1
    λS0→T1 Structural relaxation energy in T1 after transition from S0
    λT1→S0 Structural relaxation energy in T1 after transition from S0
    ∆Esolv Solvation energy at the optimized geometry in vacuum of S0
    μ Dipole moment of S0
    M Molecular mass
    Vmol Molecular volume
    B1(S0) Length of the longest side of the cuboid box enclosing a S0 molecule
    B2(S0) Length of the second longest side of the cuboid box enclosing a S0 molecule
    B3(S0) Length of the shortest side of the cuboid box enclosing a S0 molecule
